Bear Transit App

Greetings! Use this app to find the nearest Bear Transit Perimeter Line bus stop location and the next arrival time. The location box accepts the input of your location in longitude and latitude. In order to view a list of the stations, move your cursor to the map and scroll down. The time is shown in military time (ex. 7:00am = 7:00, 7:00pm = 19:00). Thankfully, we've programmed the app to automatically accept your computer's location through the browser, but if (for some weird reason) you happen to know the coordinates of a different location for which you'd like to use this app, feel free to do that.
